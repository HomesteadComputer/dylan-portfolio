The Best Movies According to Dylan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Tron: Legacy

Amazing movie made after the original Tron. Awesome plot line and captures your attention at every corner.

Pixels

Fun and Hilarious movie with Adam Sandler and other comedians alike. Was really fun the watch and made me laugh a lot.

WarGames

Fun and exciting movie that takes you through what hacking was like in 1983. Of course it's not completely accurate it is still fun to watch.
